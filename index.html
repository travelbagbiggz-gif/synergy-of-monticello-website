NeuroStar TMS Therapy
at Synergy

When antidepressants haven't worked, NeuroStar TMS Therapy offers a clinically proven, non-invasive alternative. Using precisely targeted magnetic pulses, TMS stimulates the regions of the brain responsible for mood — with no medication and no downtime.

✓ FDA-Cleared ✓ Non-Invasive ✓ No Sedation ✓ Insurance Covered ✓ 30–40 Min Sessions ✓ Same-Day Return to Work

Consultation & Brain Mapping

A full psychiatric evaluation determines candidacy. Brain mapping targets the optimal stimulation site.
