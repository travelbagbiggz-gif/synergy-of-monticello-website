## ✅ Features

- Appointment scheduling by specialty
- Full provider team profiles (14 providers)
- TMS Therapy information & clinical evidence
- **MedEnt Patient Portal** integration
- **PHQ-9 Depression Screening** (submits directly to care team)
- Insurance information (Aetna, BlueCross, Cigna, Medicare & more)
- Crisis line resources (988 Lifeline)
- Contact form powered by EmailJS
- Office hours & directions
- Mobile responsive design
- 5 mental health condition categories
